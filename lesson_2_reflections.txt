How is the staging area different from the working directory and the repository? What value do you think it offers?

The staging area is where you can add multiple files before you commit then together. It allows you
to mix and match files to commit together.

How can you use the staging area to make sure you have one commit per logical change?

You can compare the staging area and current commit to verify what your change is.

What are some situations when branches would be helpful in keeping your history organized? How would branches help?

If you have multiple branches for the same subject you could label the branches. It would help you keep track of changes.
